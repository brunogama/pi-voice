## Before opening an issue

Run:

```text
/voice doctor
```

Include the output, your OS, terminal app, Pi version, and whether you are using `whisper-cli` or an OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

Do not include API keys, Authorization headers, private audio, or sensitive dictated content.
